Erik's Day — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Erik's Day is a private app for logging daily care information for one person. It is built to keep your information under your own control.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no servers of its own and does not send your data to the developer or to any third party. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising. The app does not require an account and does not ask for any personal information.

Where your data is stored

Everything you enter is saved as files in a folder that you choose in iCloud Drive, stored under your own Apple Account. Each person who uses the app selects their own folder; families typically share one folder so several caregivers can log from their own devices. Apple's iCloud syncs that folder between the devices you have given access to. This storage and syncing is handled by Apple under your Apple Account and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

What stays on your device

The app keeps a small reference (a security-scoped bookmark) on each device so it can reopen the folder you picked. This never leaves your device.

Deleting your data

Because your data is just files in your iCloud Drive folder, you can delete any entry from within the app, or remove the whole folder from iCloud Drive at any time. There is nothing held anywhere else to delete.

Children

The app is used by caregivers to record care information. It collects no data and shows no ads or external content.

Erik's Day — Personvernerklæring

Sist oppdatert: 14. juni 2026

Erik's Day er en privat app for å registrere daglig omsorgsinformasjon for én person. Den er laget for å holde informasjonen din under din egen kontroll.

Hva vi samler inn

Ingenting. Appen har ingen egne servere og sender ikke dataene dine til utvikleren eller til noen tredjepart. Det er ingen analyse, ingen sporing og ingen reklame. Appen krever ingen konto og ber ikke om personopplysninger.

Hvor dataene lagres

Alt du legger inn lagres som filer i en mappe du velger i iCloud Drive, under din egen Apple-konto. Hver person som bruker appen velger sin egen mappe; familier deler vanligvis én mappe slik at flere omsorgspersoner kan registrere fra sine egne enheter. Apples iCloud synkroniserer mappen mellom enhetene du har gitt tilgang. Lagring og synkronisering håndteres av Apple under din Apple-konto, og er underlagt Apples personvernerklæring.
